Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Aurelia ("we", "us") provides an AI-powered aura color reading from selfies. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos: Images you capture or select are sent for AI analysis. They are processed to generate your aura reading. An image is only stored if you choose to save a reading to your history.
  • Account data: Email address (and, for social sign-in, basic profile info) used for authentication.
  • Usage data: Basic, non-identifying app activity used to improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and display your aura analysis.
  • To authenticate your account and manage subscriptions.
  • To store your saved readings so you can revisit them.

3. Third-Party Services

We share data only as needed with these providers:

  • Supabase — authentication and database.
  • OpenAI — image analysis to generate readings.
  • Cloudflare R2 — storage of images you choose to save.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.

4. Data Retention

Images used only for analysis are not permanently stored. Readings (and their images) you explicitly save remain until you delete them or your account.

5. Your Rights

You can delete any saved reading, or your entire account and associated data, from within the app settings or by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Aurelia is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data. No system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Disclaimer

Aurelia is for entertainment and self-reflection. Aura readings are AI-generated interpretations and are not scientific, medical, or psychological assessments.

Questions or data requests: hello@fabulalabs.net
